# Business Process: Global Customer Analytics and Insights

## Purpose
This process aggregates customer behavior data from multiple regions to generate business intelligence insights and predictive models for product development, marketing optimization, and revenue forecasting.

## Processing Activities

### Data Collection and Aggregation
The process collects data from three primary assets:
- **US Customer Database** (hosted in us-east-1): Contains UserID, PurchaseHistory, BrowsingBehavior, and PaymentInformation for US-based customers.
- **EU Customer Database** (hosted in eu-west-1): Contains UserID, PurchaseHistory, BrowsingBehavior, and PaymentInformation for EU-based customers.
- **APAC Customer Database** (hosted in ap-southeast-1): Contains UserID, PurchaseHistory, BrowsingBehavior, and PaymentInformation for APAC-based customers.

### Cross-Border Data Transfer
All regional data is transferred to the **Global Analytics Platform** (hosted in us-east-1) for centralized processing. This involves:
- Transfer of EU customer data from eu-west-1 to us-east-1 (requires Standard Contractual Clauses under GDPR Article 46)
- Transfer of APAC customer data from ap-southeast-1 to us-east-1 (requires adequacy assessment)

### Third-Party Data Sharing
The aggregated analytics are shared with:
- **MarketInsights Corp** (Data Processor): Receives anonymized PurchaseHistory and BrowsingBehavior to generate market trend reports
- **PredictiveAI Solutions** (Data Processor): Receives pseudonymized customer data including Age, Gender, LocationData, and PurchaseHistory to build predictive models

### Data Elements Processed
- Email Address
- Full Name
- Home Address
- IP Address
- Device ID
- Purchase History
- Browsing History
- Location Data
- Age
- Gender
- Payment Information

### Data Subject Types
- Customers (End Users)
- Prospects (Potential Customers)
- Trial Users

## Legal Basis
- Legitimate Interest (for business analytics)
- Consent (for third-party sharing with MarketInsights Corp and PredictiveAI Solutions)

## Retention Period
- Raw customer data: 3 years from last interaction
- Aggregated analytics: 7 years for business reporting
- Shared data with third parties: Deleted within 90 days of contract termination
