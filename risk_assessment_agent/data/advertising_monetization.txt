# Business Process: Targeted Advertising and Data Monetization

## Purpose
This process leverages user behavioral data to deliver personalized advertisements and generates revenue by selling anonymized audience segments to advertising partners and data brokers.

## Processing Activities

### Data Collection
Primary data sources:
- **Mobile App Analytics Platform**: Collects DeviceID, AppUsageData, LocationData, and InAppPurchaseHistory
- **Web Analytics System**: Collects Cookies, BrowsingHistory, SearchHistory, IPAddress, and ClickstreamData
- **Social Media Integration API**: Collects SocialMediaActivity, UserInterests, and DemographicInformation

### Profile Building
The **User Profiling Engine** (hosted in us-west-2) combines data from all sources to create comprehensive user profiles containing:
- Full Name
- Email Address
- Age
- Gender
- Location Data
- Interests and Preferences
- Purchase History
- Browsing Behavior
- Social Media Activity

### Audience Segmentation
Profiles are segmented into targetable audiences based on:
- Demographics (Age, Gender, Location)
- Behavioral patterns (Purchase frequency, Product preferences)
- Psychographic attributes (Interests, Lifestyle indicators)

### Third-Party Data Sales
Anonymized audience segments are sold to:
- **AdTech Partners Inc**: Receives anonymized audience segments with Age, Gender, LocationData, and InterestCategories
- **DataBroker Global**: Receives pseudonymized datasets including BrowsingHistory, PurchaseHistory, and DemographicInformation
- **Marketing Cloud Services**: Receives email lists (EmailAddress) with associated interest categories for targeted campaigns

### Cross-Border Transfers
- User profile data from EU users is transferred from eu-central-1 to us-west-2 for processing
- Anonymized segments are distributed to partners globally, including:
  - AdTech Partners Inc (US-based)
  - DataBroker Global (UK-based)
  - Marketing Cloud Services (Singapore-based)

- App Users
- Social Media Followers

## Legal Basis
- Consent (for targeted advertising and data sales)
- Legitimate Interest (for analytics and product improvement)

## Retention Period
- Raw behavioral data: 2 years from collection
- User profiles: Active until user account deletion or opt-out
- Sold data segments: Buyer must delete within 1 year per contractual terms

## Privacy Controls
- Users can opt-out of data sales via Privacy Settings Dashboard
- Users can request deletion of their profile data
- Anonymization applied before data transfer to third parties (k-anonymity threshold of 100)
