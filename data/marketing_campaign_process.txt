BUSINESS PROCESS: Personalized Marketing Campaign Management
==============================================================

Process Owner: Marketing Operations Team
Last Updated: November 2025
Regulatory Framework: GDPR, CCPA, CAN-SPAM, CASL

PROCESS OVERVIEW
----------------
This process describes how we create, execute, and optimize personalized marketing 
campaigns across email, SMS, and digital advertising channels using customer data 
and behavioral analytics.

DATA ASSETS USED
----------------

1. CUSTOMER DATA PLATFORM (CDP-MAIN)
   Location: Segment CDP (AWS us-west-2)
   Purpose: Unified customer profiles and segmentation
   Personal Data Stored:
   - Customer ID (unique identifier)
   - Email Address (campaign targeting)
   - Phone Number (SMS campaigns)
   - Full Name (personalization)
   - Age Range (demographic targeting)
   - Gender (demographic targeting)
   - Location (city, state, country - geo-targeting)
   - Language Preference (content localization)
   - Income Range (estimated, for targeting)
   - Household Composition (estimated)
   
   Legal Basis: Consent (marketing), Legitimate interest (customer relationship)
   Retention Period: Until consent withdrawn or 3 years of inactivity

2. BEHAVIORAL ANALYTICS DATABASE (ANALYTICS-DB)
   Location: Google BigQuery (us-central1)
   Purpose: Track customer interactions and behavior
   Personal Data Collected:
   - Customer ID (linkage to profile)
   - Website Visit History (pages viewed, time spent)
   - Product Browsing History (items viewed, searched)
   - Purchase History (products bought, amounts, dates)
   - Email Engagement (opens, clicks, unsubscribes)
   - App Usage Data (features used, session duration)
   - Device Information (type, OS, browser)
   - IP Address (location inference, fraud detection)
   - Cookie IDs (cross-device tracking)
   
   Legal Basis: Consent (cookies), Legitimate interest (service improvement)
   Retention Period: 2 years from collection

3. EMAIL MARKETING PLATFORM (EMAIL-PLATFORM)
   Provider: Mailchimp
   Purpose: Send personalized email campaigns
   Personal Data Shared:
   - Email Address
   - First Name, Last Name
   - Customer Segment (e.g., "High Value", "At Risk")
   - Purchase History Summary
   - Email Preferences
   - Engagement Scores
   
   Legal Basis: Consent (explicit opt-in required)
   Data Processing: Mailchimp acts as processor
   Opt-out: Unsubscribe link in every email

4. SMS MARKETING SERVICE (SMS-GATEWAY)
   Provider: Twilio
   Purpose: Send promotional SMS messages
   Personal Data Shared:
   - Phone Number
   - First Name
   - Customer Segment
   - SMS Opt-in Status
   - SMS Opt-in Date
   
   Legal Basis: Explicit consent (double opt-in)
   Compliance: TCPA, CTIA guidelines
   Opt-out: STOP keyword processing

5. ADVERTISING PLATFORMS
   
   a) GOOGLE ADS (GOOGLE-ADS-ACCOUNT)
      Purpose: Display and search advertising
      Personal Data Shared:
      - Hashed Email Addresses (Customer Match)
      - Phone Numbers (hashed)
      - Customer Segments (for Similar Audiences)
      
      Legal Basis: Legitimate interest, Consent (cookies)
      Data Matching: Google matches hashed data to user accounts

   b) FACEBOOK ADS (META-ADS-ACCOUNT)
      Purpose: Social media advertising
      Personal Data Shared:
      - Hashed Email Addresses (Custom Audiences)
      - Hashed Phone Numbers
      - Customer Segments
      - Lookalike Audience Seeds
      
      Legal Basis: Legitimate interest, Consent (cookies)
      Data Matching: Meta matches hashed data to user accounts

   c) LINKEDIN ADS (LINKEDIN-ADS-ACCOUNT)
      Purpose: B2B advertising
      Personal Data Shared:
      - Work Email Addresses (hashed)
      - Company Name
      - Job Title (if available)
      
      Legal Basis: Legitimate interest (B2B marketing)

6. PREDICTIVE ANALYTICS ENGINE (ML-PREDICT)
   Location: AWS SageMaker (us-east-1)
   Purpose: Predict customer behavior and preferences
   Personal Data Processed:
   - Customer ID
   - Purchase History
   - Browsing Behavior
   - Email Engagement History
   - Demographic Data
   - Predicted Lifetime Value (calculated)
   - Churn Risk Score (calculated)
   - Product Affinity Scores (calculated)
   
   Legal Basis: Legitimate interest (business optimization)
   Automated Decision: Yes - campaign targeting decisions
   Human Review: Available upon request

7. A/B TESTING PLATFORM (OPTIMIZE-PLATFORM)
   Provider: Optimizely
   Purpose: Test campaign variations
   Personal Data Processed:
   - Anonymous User ID (cookie-based)
   - Test Variant Assignment
   - Conversion Events
   - Session Behavior
   
   Legal Basis: Consent (cookies), Legitimate interest
   Anonymization: Personal identifiers removed after 30 days

8. CONSENT MANAGEMENT PLATFORM (CMP-SYSTEM)
   Location: OneTrust (cloud-hosted)
   Purpose: Manage marketing consent and preferences
   Personal Data Stored:
   - Customer ID
   - Email Address
   - Consent Records (what, when, how obtained)
   - Preference Center Selections
   - Opt-out Requests
   - Communication Channel Preferences
   
   Legal Basis: Legal obligation (consent records)
   Retention Period: 7 years (proof of consent)

PROCESS FLOW
------------

Step 1: Audience Segmentation
- Marketing team defines target audience criteria
- Query CDP-MAIN for matching customers
- Apply filters: demographics, behavior, purchase history
- Data used: All customer profile and behavioral data
- Purpose: Identify relevant audience for campaign

Step 2: Predictive Scoring
- ML-PREDICT generates scores for target audience
- Scores calculated: Conversion probability, churn risk, LTV
- Data used: Historical behavior, purchase patterns
- Purpose: Prioritize high-value prospects, optimize spend

Step 3: Consent Verification
- Check CMP-SYSTEM for marketing consent status
- Filter out customers who haven't consented
- Verify channel-specific consent (email vs SMS)
- Data used: Consent records, opt-out lists
- Purpose: Ensure compliance with consent requirements

Step 4: Personalization
- Generate personalized content for each recipient
- Data used: Name, purchase history, browsing behavior, preferences
- Variables: Product recommendations, discount offers, content topics
- Purpose: Increase engagement and conversion rates

Step 5: Campaign Execution

Email Campaign:
- Upload audience to EMAIL-PLATFORM (Mailchimp)
- Data shared: Email, name, segment, personalization variables
- Emails sent with tracking pixels
- Purpose: Deliver marketing message

SMS Campaign:
- Upload audience to SMS-GATEWAY (Twilio)
- Data shared: Phone number, name, message content
- SMS sent with opt-out instructions
- Purpose: Deliver time-sensitive offers

Digital Advertising:
- Upload hashed customer lists to ad platforms
- Google Ads: Create Customer Match audiences
- Facebook Ads: Create Custom Audiences
- LinkedIn Ads: Create Matched Audiences
- Purpose: Retarget customers, create lookalike audiences

Step 6: Performance Tracking
- Track campaign performance in ANALYTICS-DB
- Metrics: Opens, clicks, conversions, revenue
- Data collected: Engagement events, conversion events
- Link back to customer profiles in CDP-MAIN
- Purpose: Measure ROI, optimize future campaigns

Step 7: A/B Testing Analysis
- OPTIMIZE-PLATFORM tracks test performance
- Compare variants: subject lines, content, offers, timing
- Statistical significance calculated
- Winner selected for broader rollout
- Purpose: Continuous improvement

Step 8: Attribution and Reporting
- Multi-touch attribution across channels
- Data sources: ANALYTICS-DB, EMAIL-PLATFORM, ad platforms
- Customer journey reconstruction
- Revenue attribution by touchpoint
- Purpose: Understand marketing effectiveness

INDIVIDUAL RIGHTS
-----------------

Right to Access: Customers can view their profile data in account settings
Right to Rectification: Update preferences in preference center
Right to Erasure: Request deletion via privacy@company.com
Right to Restrict Processing: Opt-out of specific marketing channels
Right to Data Portability: Export marketing data in JSON format
Right to Object: Opt-out links in all marketing communications
Automated Decision-Making: Customers can request human review of targeting decisions

CONSENT MANAGEMENT
------------------

Consent Collection:
- Explicit opt-in required for email marketing
- Double opt-in required for SMS marketing
- Cookie consent banner for behavioral tracking
- Granular consent options (email, SMS, phone, mail)

Consent Records:
- What: Specific consent given (e.g., "email marketing")
- When: Timestamp of consent
- How: Method of collection (e.g., "signup form", "preference center")
- Version: Privacy policy version at time of consent

Withdrawal:
- Unsubscribe links in all emails
- STOP keyword for SMS
- Preference center for granular control
- Processing time: Immediate (automated)

SECURITY MEASURES
-----------------

Technical Measures:
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Hashing before sharing with ad platforms
- Access controls and audit logging
- Regular security assessments
- Data loss prevention (DLP) tools

Organizational Measures:
- Marketing team training on privacy
- Data minimization principles
- Regular consent audits
- Vendor risk assessments
- Privacy by design in campaign planning

COMPLIANCE NOTES
----------------

GDPR Compliance:
- Legal basis documented (consent or legitimate interest)
- Consent freely given, specific, informed, unambiguous
- Easy withdrawal of consent
- Data minimization applied
- Privacy notices at collection points
- DPIA completed for profiling activities

CCPA Compliance:
- Privacy policy discloses marketing data use
- "Do Not Sell" respected (we don't sell data)
- Opt-out of targeted advertising available
- Right to know what data is collected
- Right to delete marketing data

CAN-SPAM Compliance (US):
- Accurate header information
- Clear subject lines
- Physical address in emails
- Unsubscribe mechanism in every email
- Honor opt-outs within 10 business days

CASL Compliance (Canada):
- Express consent required for commercial emails
- Identification of sender
- Unsubscribe mechanism
- Honor opt-outs within 10 business days

TCPA Compliance (US - SMS):
- Prior express written consent for SMS
- Clear disclosure of message frequency
- STOP keyword processing
- HELP keyword support

RISKS AND MITIGATION
--------------------

Risk: Sending marketing to opted-out customers
Mitigation: Real-time consent checks, suppression lists, automated opt-out processing

Risk: Excessive profiling and discrimination
Mitigation: Fairness reviews, avoid sensitive categories, human oversight

Risk: Data breach exposing customer preferences
Mitigation: Encryption, access controls, vendor security assessments

Risk: Non-compliance with consent requirements
Mitigation: Consent management platform, regular audits, training

Risk: Unauthorized data sharing with ad platforms
Mitigation: Data processing agreements, hashing, minimal data sharing

Risk: Customer complaints about irrelevant marketing
Mitigation: Preference centers, frequency caps, relevance scoring
